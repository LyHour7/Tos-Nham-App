Multi-Branch Restaurant Management System

Tos Nham App

A Flutter mobile application for restaurant customers and staff: branch browsing, table reservations, QR check-in, online ordering, payment tracking, and customer reviews.

Presentation Deck
Project Problem

Restaurant operations need a single digital flow

  • Manual reservations can create fake bookings and wasted tables.
  • Customers need a simple way to browse, reserve, order, and pay.
  • Staff need fast access to reservations, orders, and payment status.
  • Multi-branch restaurants need branch-specific menus and operations.
  • Payment confirmation should be visible to the team.
  • Customer feedback should update the menu experience immediately.
Why This App Matters
Solution

A mobile-first restaurant management platform

Customers Browse branches and menus, add items to cart, book tables, pay deposits, and track orders.
Staff Scan reservation QR codes, manage reservations, view orders, and update order status.
Admins Manage branches, menus, payments, reservations, online orders, and reports.

2System calculates 50 percent deposit
3Customer pays using KHQR flow
4Reservation QR code is generated
5Staff scans QR when customer arrives
Table Booking
Online Ordering

Cart, checkout, payment, and status tracking

  • Customers select menu items from one branch.
  • The app validates item availability and quantities.
  • Orders can be paid using KHQR or handled with cash flow.
  • Staff views incoming orders in the operational dashboard.
  • Order status moves through pending, preparing, ready, and completed.
  • Payment receipt state supports pending, verified, and rejected.

Recent Improvement

Realtime menu rating summary

  • Before: review submission worked, but the average rating stayed stale until refresh.
  • Cause: the screen updated the review list but did not refresh the displayed aggregate rating.
  • Impact: users saw inconsistent rating count and average value.
  • Now: after submit, the screen awaits the latest detail data.
  • The UI syncs average rating and total ratings from the API.
  • If aggregate fields are missing, the screen computes them from review ratings.
